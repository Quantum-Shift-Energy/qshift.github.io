Quantum Shift publishes analysis on energy systems, infrastructure markets, and operational dynamics. The focus is on how physical systems and economic structures interact — grid infrastructure, energy transition, industrial policy, and the operational realities that determine what is actually buildable.

## Scope

- **Energy systems** — electricity markets, grid infrastructure, capacity planning, transition pathways
- **Infrastructure markets** — how capital flows through physical infrastructure, pricing, and regulatory frameworks
- **Operational analysis** — engineering and economic constraints that shape what gets built and how it performs

## Approach

Evidence-first. Models are tools for thinking, not substitutes for it. Where data is unavailable or contested, that's noted explicitly.

See the [Method](/method/) page for more on analytical approach.
